# Capture GO Support

Welcome to Capture GO support! We are here to ensure you have the best experience.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**What is the goal of the game?**
In Capture GO, the first player to capture one or more of the opponent's stones wins the game. It's a fast-paced variant of traditional Go.

**Is the game free?**
Yes! You can play against AI and Online opponents for free. Life stones refill automatically over time.

**How do I collect Shapes?**
Shapes are patterns formed by stones on the board. When you create a recognized shape during a game, it is automatically added to your Collection Album.
