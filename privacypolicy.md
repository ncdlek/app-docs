# Privacy Policy for Capture GO

**Effective Date:** January 13, 2026

Capture GO ("we," "us," or "our") values your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

## 1. Information We Collect
*   **Gameplay Data:** We store game history, moves, and match results to provide the replay feature and statistics.
*   **Account Info:** Anonymous IDs are created to sync your progress (Streaks, Collections) across sessions. If you sign in (e.g., Apple Sign-In), we link your progress to that account.
*   **Analytics:** We use aggregated, anonymous data to understand which features are popular and to improve app stability.

## 2. Online Multiplayer
*   **Usernames:** A random or chosen username is visible to opponents during online matches.
*   **Match Data:** Online moves are relayed via our secure servers to synchronize the game state.

## 3. Data Security
We do not sell your personal data. All connections are secured using industry-standard encryption.

## 4. Contact
For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please contact: engin@yazilan.org
